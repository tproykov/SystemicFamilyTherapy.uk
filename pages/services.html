Services - Systemic and Family Therapy with Dr Proykov

I provide specialised support for families, couples, and individuals through evidence-based systemic therapy approaches. I offer a comprehensive range of online therapeutic services including individual therapy sessions, family therapy sessions, couple therapy, online consultations, and clinical supervision.

Online Therapy Sessions

All therapy sessions are conducted online via secure GDPR-compliant video platform.

Family therapy with separated parents experiencing child contact difficulties

This highly specialised type of therapy is aimed to:

  • Improve the communication between parents
  • Address difficulties in the child contact
  • Support parents in responding to children's feelings and behaviours
  • Work on strategies of both parents meeting their own emotional needs

Family Therapy

  • Family conflict resolution
  • Improving parent-child relationships
  • Dealing with life transitions
  • Supporting families through separation/divorce
  • Supporting separated families make arrangements for the children
  • Blended family integration

Sessions: 60 minutes | Available evenings and weekends

Couple Therapy

Specialised support for couples facing challenges or seeking to strengthen their relationship:

  • Communication difficulties
  • Trust rebuilding
  • Conflict resolution
  • Pre-marital counseling
  • Life transition support

Sessions: 60 minutes | Available evenings and weekends

Individual Therapy

Personal therapeutic support using systemic approaches for:

  • Anxiety and depression
  • Relationship difficulties
  • Emotional regulation
  • Personal development
  • Life transitions
  • Work-related stress

Sessions: 50 minutes | Available evenings and weekends

Professional Supervision

  • Trainee family therapists
  • Qualified therapists
  • Mental health professionals

Sessions: 50 minutes | Individual or group supervision available evenings and weekends

Booking Information

Initial consultations over the phone are available to discuss your needs and determine the most appropriate approach. All sessions are conducted online via a secure, GDPR-compliant platform.

For rates and availability, please contact me. Information how to do so you can find on the Contacts page.
