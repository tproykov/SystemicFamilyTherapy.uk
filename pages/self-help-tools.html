Communication Tools

Active Listening Exercise

Practice these steps to improve your listening skills:

  1. Give full attention to the speaker
  2. Observe non-verbal cues
  3. Avoid interrupting
  4. Reflect back what you've heard
  5. Ask clarifying questions

Conflict Resolution Template

A structured approach to addressing conflicts:

  • State the issue clearly
  • Express feelings using "I" statements
  • Listen to other perspectives
  • Brainstorm solutions together
  • Agree on action steps
